Rice and Meat

  • Rice: 200g
  • Beef: 150g
  • Onion: 1 medium
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  1. Wash the rice and soak it for 20 minutes. Drain well.
  2. Boil 2 cups of chicken broth. Add rice, cover, and cook on low for 15 minutes. Let it cool.
  3. Cut the beef into small cubes. Add salt and pepper.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan. Cook the beef for 3-4 minutes until brown. Take it out.
  5. Add chopped onion and garlic to the pan. Cook for 2 minutes until soft.
  6. Add peas and carrots. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Add the rice, soy sauce, honey, and ginger. Stir for 3 minutes.
  8. Put the beef back in. Move rice to the side. Scramble eggs in the pan, then mix with rice and beef.
  9. Top with green onions. Serve hot.

Carbohydrate: 45g

Protein: 22g

Fat: 12g

Lentil Soup

  • Red lentils: 150g
  • Carrot: 1 medium
  • Onion: 1 medium
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Water: 1 liter
  • Salt: 1 tsp
  • Pepper: 1/2 tsp
  1. Rinse lentils thoroughly under cold water.
  2. Chop carrot and onion into small pieces.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pot and sauté onion and carrot for 3 minutes.
  4. Add the lentils and stir for another minute.
  5. Pour in the water, bring to a boil, and reduce to a simmer.
  6. Add salt and pepper, then cook for 20–25 minutes until lentils are soft.
  7. Blend the soup for a smooth texture, or leave chunky as desired.
  8. Serve hot, optionally garnished with fresh herbs or a squeeze of lemon.

Carbohydrate: 28g

Protein: 9g

Fat: 6g
